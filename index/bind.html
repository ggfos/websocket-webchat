KeeeWeee

KeeeWeee is a free social & IM App that allows users to find, learn & chat with people with common interests. It integrates various social network users together. Users can chat with each other regardless of the social networks they are from. For example, a Facebook user can talk to a Twitter user. A Twitter user can talk to a LinkedIn user. Or the three of them can talk in a group or circle.
